Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2024

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Amazon Review Analyzer extension.

1. Extension Overview

Amazon Review Analyzer (also known as Amarize Extension) is a Chrome extension that helps analyze and summarize product reviews on Amazon using artificial intelligence APIs like Perplexity and OpenAI.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store your personal information on our servers. All data is stored locally in your browser, including:

  • API keys (for Perplexity, OpenAI, or custom services) that you provide
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Cache of previously generated review summaries

3. How We Use Information

The extension uses the collected information to:

  • Send requests to Perplexity/OpenAI APIs to analyze product reviews
  • Store generated summaries for future use without requiring additional API requests
  • Customize the user experience based on saved settings

4. Extension Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • Tabs permission: To identify the current URL and Amazon product ID
  • Storage permission: To store your settings and cache summaries
  • Host permissions: To connect to Perplexity/OpenAI APIs and Amazon websites

5. API Key Security

Your API keys are stored locally in your browser through Chrome's Storage API. We do not send, store, or have access to your API keys on our servers.

6. Product Review Data

The extension collects publicly available reviews from Amazon to send to the analysis API. We do not store these reviews outside of your browser and only use them to generate summaries.

7. Third Parties

The extension uses the following third-party services:

  • Perplexity AI API: For analyzing product reviews. Please see Perplexity's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API: For analyzing product reviews. Please see OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to our practices. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
