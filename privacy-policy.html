Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2024

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how the Amazon Review Analyzer Pro extension collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Introduction

Amazon Review Analyzer Pro is a Chrome extension that helps users analyze and summarize Amazon product reviews using artificial intelligence through services like Perplexity API and OpenAI API.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers. All data processing occurs locally within your browser:

  • API keys that you provide for AI services (Perplexity, OpenAI, or custom endpoints)
  • Extension settings and user preferences
  • Cached review summaries (stored locally to prevent redundant API calls)
  • Amazon product information and publicly available review content that you choose to analyze

3. Data Usage

The extension uses information solely to:

  • Analyze and summarize Amazon product reviews by sending review text to the AI service you've configured
  • Store generated summaries locally for faster access when revisiting products
  • Customize your experience based on your saved preferences
  • Enable basic extension functionality

4. Required Permissions

To provide its functionality, the extension requires these Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab/tabs: To identify the current Amazon product page and gather review content
  • storage: To store your settings, API keys, and cached summaries locally
  • host permissions (amazon.com and related domains): To access and analyze product reviews
  • host permissions (AI service endpoints): To communicate with Perplexity/OpenAI APIs for review analysis

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function properly.

5. API Key Security

Your API keys are:

  • Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure Storage API
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Only sent directly to the respective AI service when you request review analysis
  • Fully under your control - you can remove them at any time

6. Review Data Processing

When you analyze a product:

  • Publicly available Amazon review content is sent to the AI service you've configured
  • This data is processed according to the respective AI service's terms and policies
  • Review content is only sent when you explicitly request analysis
  • Results are cached locally in your browser to improve performance

7. Third-Party Services

The extension integrates with these third-party services:

  • Perplexity AI: When using Perplexity for analysis, review data is processed according to Perplexity's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: When using OpenAI for analysis, review data is processed according to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • ExtensionPay: For processing payments and managing subscriptions. When you purchase a subscription, payment information is processed by ExtensionPay according to their privacy information available on their website. As stated in their FAQ, they collect your email address and store payment information securely in Stripe. We do not receive or store your complete payment details.
  • Amazon: The extension operates on Amazon's website to collect publicly available review content

Please note that these third-party services have their own privacy policies and terms of service that govern how they handle your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or process information from children under 13.

9. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Data retention is under your control
  • You can clear cached summaries through the extension settings
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all locally stored data

For subscription information, ExtensionPay retains payment data according to their privacy policy and legal requirements.

10. Your Rights

You have full control over your data and can:

  • View, edit, or delete your API keys and settings at any time
  • Clear cached summaries
  • Manage your subscription through ExtensionPay
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
